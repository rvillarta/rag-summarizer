(ECNS) -- China's State Council Information Office will host a press conference at 10 a.m. on Wednesday to outline preparations for the upcoming military parade, according to Xinhua News Agency.
Two senior military officials, Wu Zeke and Xu Guizhong, will present details on the parade arrangements and address questions from the press.
