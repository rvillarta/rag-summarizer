The 12th World Games has kicked off in Chengdu, Sichuan Province.
Apart from watching the games, visitors can play Mahjong in the water, savor local signature delights, spend a leisurely day sipping traditional Gaiwan tea, experience the marvelous art of Sichuan Opera face-changing, or watch cute pandas playing at the breeding centers. With open arms, Chengdu invites you to explore its unique charms!
