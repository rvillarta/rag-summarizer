"Infants and children 6 through 23 months of age are at the highest risk for severe COVID-19," the AAP states in its release. The AAP also recommends a "single dose of age-appropriate COVID-19 vaccine" for kids and teens 2 and older who are at high risk of severe COVID, have never been vaccinated before, and who live with people who are at a high risk of severe disease. The CDC no longer recommends the COVID-19 vaccine for children, but a major medical group is going against that guidance.
